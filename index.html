Popup Snake extension!

A simple, free extension which can be added to your chrome browser. It allows you to play the authentic snake game anytime, anywhere! Just click the snake icon in the top right, and you're good to go!

Instructions:
A box will pop up with a snake and an apple in it. Move the snake using the arrow keys, and try to eat as many apples as possible, without hitting the edges or even your own tail!

Remember to not press many buttons at one time, as the clicks may not get picked up.

Clicker champions!

A simple game where you can compete with your friends, to see who is the best at clicking buttons!

Instructions:
There's two modes: pure clicking, and reaction clicker. In pure clicking its simple. Just click your mouse as many times you can! You'll be able to see your score at the end.
In reaction clicker you have to have fast reactions. Click the key shown on screen. Don't click the others though! You'll lose a point.

As in all our games, the high scores are stored, so you won't have to keep track!
