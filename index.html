Who we are

Kristian Games™ is currently a non-profit foundation founded by a teenager named Kristian. Coding is simply his passion, and all the work done here is just for fun. Some of the work we do might not seem that professional, because it isn't, and that is not our goal. If you have any suggestions, please send them here.
